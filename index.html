LODGING

Invitees will be arriving at Grantley Adams International Airport. Invitees will then stay at The House by Elegant Hotels set on a pristine stretch of white sand on Barbados' West Coast, The House is the beachside version of a hip downtown hotel.  

Elegant Hotels provides comfortable and affordable luxury resort accommodation on the sun-drenched island of Barbados,St. Lucia & Antigua. Elegant Hotels is one of the leading Caribbean hotel groups and manages nine distinctly unique, Caribbean luxury resorts located on superb beachfront property.  

Elegant Hotels was formed in 1998, bringing together seven of the most luxurious and well-known hotels in Barbados. Elegant Hotels consists of four outstanding Breakfast Plan resorts--Colony Club Hotel, Tamarind Hotel, Treasure Beach and the intimate House--along with three of the best all-inclusive properties in the Caribbean, Turtle Beach Resort, Crystal Cove Hotel and Waves Hotel & Spa. The collection expanded into the Caribbean in 2017 with a sales and management contract for The Landings Resort & Spa St. Lucia and coming soon Hodges Bay Resort & Spa Antigua.
